PhD Candidate at University of California, Berkeley

I am a 6th year PhD student in the department of Civil and Environmental Engineering. Originally from Houston, Texas, I received my BSc in Civil Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin in 2019. I currently focus on surface water and groundwater interactions around human-constructed reservoirs using CYGNSS and GRACE satellite data. Outside of research, I enjoy hiking with friends, windsurfing, and hanging out with my cat.
